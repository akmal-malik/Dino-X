The Swift Velociraptor

The Velociraptor was a small but fast and intelligent carnivorous dinosaur that lived during the late Cretaceous period, around 75 to 71 million years ago. It was known for its speed, agility, and sharp curved claws.

Some Facts About Velociraptor:

  • It grew up to about 6.8 feet long and stood around 1.6 feet tall at the hip.
  • It had a long, stiff tail that helped it balance while running.
  • Each foot had a large curved claw used for hunting and gripping prey.
  • Velociraptors were likely covered in feathers and may have hunted in groups.

Scientists believe that Velociraptors were among the most intelligent dinosaurs, using coordinated tactics to hunt their prey. Despite their small size, their speed, agility, and sharp claws made them one of the most effective predators of the Cretaceous period.
