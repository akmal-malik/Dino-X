About DinoEvolution

DinoEvolution is a creative educational project designed to take visitors on a visual and informational journey through the history of dinosaurs. This page explains our purpose, inspiration, and the team behind the creation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make learning about prehistoric life exciting and visually engaging. We aim to show how dinosaurs evolved over millions of years, adapting to changing environments and dominating the Earth long before humans appeared.

How This Project Was Built

DinoEvolution was created using core web technologies — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — with a focus on responsive design and smooth interactivity. Every image, color, and animation is crafted to reflect the mysterious and powerful atmosphere of the Mesozoic era.

Why Dinosaurs?

Dinosaurs represent one of the most fascinating parts of Earth’s history. They teach us about evolution, survival, and adaptation. Through this project, we bring together science and storytelling to help viewers understand how these incredible creatures once ruled the planet.
