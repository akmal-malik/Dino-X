The Armored Ankylosaurus

The Ankylosaurus was one of the most heavily armored dinosaurs that lived during the late Cretaceous period, around 68 to 66 million years ago. It was covered in thick, bony plates and had a massive club-like tail used for defense.

Some Facts About Ankylosaurus:

  • It could grow up to 26 feet long and weigh around 6 tons.
  • Its body was covered with tough armor plates called osteoderms.
  • The tail club could deliver powerful blows to predators like T-Rex.
  • It was a herbivore that fed on low-growing plants and ferns.

Scientists believe that Ankylosaurus relied on its armor and tail for protection rather than speed. Its strong defense system and peaceful nature made it one of the best-protected herbivores of the dinosaur era and a fascinating example of prehistoric evolution.
